Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ki-Liner?

Ki-Liner is a discreet, ultra-thin liner designed to sit at the bottom of a cup and add an extra layer of protection in social environments without changing the drinking experience.

How does Ki-Liner work?

Ki-Liner is designed to interact at the surface level within the drink environment, helping to disrupt or limit unwanted alterations while remaining invisible to the user.

Will Ki-Liner affect the taste or smell of my drink?

No. Ki-Liner is engineered to be neutral, meaning it does not change the taste, smell, or appearance of your drink.

Is Ki-Liner visible inside the cup?

Ki-Liner is transparent and sits flat at the bottom of the cup, making it virtually unnoticeable during normal use.

Who can use Ki-Liner?

Ki-Liner is designed for individuals, bars, nightclubs, event organizers, and institutions looking to add an extra layer of safety in social settings.

Can I order Ki-Liner in bulk for my business or event?

Yes. Secura Labs offers bulk ordering and partnership opportunities for venues, organizations, and distributors. You can contact us directly for custom pricing.
